Dutch Baby Recipe

A fancy poofed pancake. Eat savory or sweet. Or both!

This is an extremely simple recipe. Don't be fooled by all the recipes on the internet. For a good poof, follow this one.

Ingredients for a Full Size

  • 4 Room Temperature Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Cup of Warm Milk
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter
  • Nutmeg and Cinnamon to taste
  • Dollop of Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat cast iron pan on medium-low.
  2. Mix eggs in a bowl.
  3. Mix Milk with the eggs.
  4. Mix in the flour, vanilla and seasonings until no clumps remain.
  5. Add butter to the warm pan.
  6. Pour batter into the middle of the pan.
  7. Turn off burner and add to oven using a hot pad.
  8. Turn on the oven to 375degF and cook for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove and serve with Syrup or other toppings.
