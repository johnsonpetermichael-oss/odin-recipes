Chicken Rama Recipe

Tasty Thai Dish

Simple recipe. Lots of ways to spin.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • 2 tbsp Red Curry Paste
  • Garlic to taste
  • 1 tbsp Ginger
  • 1 tsp Brown Sugar
  • Half a juiced lime
  • 1 Can of Coconut Milk or Cream
  • 2 Cups of Jasmine Rice
  • Spice peppers to taste
  • Whatever protein you want however you want

Steps

  1. Sautee garlic, ginger, and curry paste
  2. Add coconut cream, sugar, spices, peppers

  4. Chop broccoli. Add at the end
