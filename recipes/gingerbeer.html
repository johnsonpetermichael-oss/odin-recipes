Ginger Beer Recipe

A delightful treat

Refreshing, easy to make beverage so long as you make a ginger bug.

Ingredients

  • Half a cup of ginger bug
  • 2 Liters of filtered water
  • 75 grams of shredded ginger
  • 2/3 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Add water, sugar, and ginger
  2. Boil 5 minutes
  3. Let cool to room temperature and add bug
  4. Fill flip top bottles and burp daily until desired carbonation is reached
